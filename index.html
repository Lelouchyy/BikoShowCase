About Bikoman

Traditional Filipino Delicacies

At Bikoman, we specialize in creating authentic Filipino rice cakes using traditional recipes passed down through generations. Our commitment to quality ingredients and time-honored cooking methods ensures every bite delivers the true taste of Filipino heritage.


Located in Zone II, Brgy. Kiskis Alcala Pangasinan, we take pride in serving our local community with freshly made delicacies. Each product is carefully crafted with love and attention to detail, using only the finest coconut milk, glutinous rice, and natural ingredients.


Pickup Only: All orders are available for pickup at our location. We believe in maintaining the freshness and quality of our products by ensuring they reach you at their best.
